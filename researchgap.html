Research Gap

Key Gaps in Existing Research

1. Adaptive Watering

  • Systems use fixed rules, lacking real-time learning.
  • Adaptive models can improve efficiency.

2. Energy Efficiency

  • Limited use of renewable energy (e.g., solar).
  • Optimized power management is needed.

3. Scalability

  • Focus is on small setups, not large farms.
  • Robust infrastructure is needed for scalability.

4. Nutrient Monitoring

  • Studies track moisture but not soil nutrients.
  • Nutrient sensors can improve plant health.

Addressing these gaps will enhance smart irrigation systems.
