Objectives

  • Automated Irrigation: Implement an intelligent system that autonomously waters plants based on real-time soil moisture readings, ensuring optimal hydration.
  • Efficient Water Management: Develop a smart water distribution mechanism that minimizes wastage and prevents overwatering through precise control.
  • IoT-Based Remote Monitoring: Enable users to seamlessly monitor and control the irrigation system from anywhere via a cloud-connected mobile or web application.
  • AI-Powered Decision Making: Integrate machine learning models to analyze environmental factors and optimize watering schedules for improved plant health and resource efficiency.
