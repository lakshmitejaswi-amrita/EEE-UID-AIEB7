Methodology

Our approach involves the integration of a soil moisture sensor with a microcontroller (Arduino UNO) that continuously monitors the moisture level of the soil. When the soil dries beyond a threshold, a signal is sent to activate a water pump, delivering water to the plants.

The components are connected in such a way that the sensor data is read at regular intervals. Based on the moisture value, a decision is made to water the plant or keep the pump off. The system is powered using a 5V source and mounted on a simple frame for practical deployment.

Additional features such as an LED indicator and serial monitoring via the Arduino IDE allow real-time debugging and status updates.
