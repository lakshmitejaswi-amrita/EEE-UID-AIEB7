Components

  • Microcontroller: ESP32
    • Serves as the central processing unit.
    • Provides robust computational capabilities and flexible GPIO features.
    • Enables reliable Wi-Fi connectivity for communication between sensors and actuators.
  • Rain Sensor
    • Detects rain and provides real-time updates on weather conditions.
    • Helps pause irrigation if rainfall is detected.
    • Warns users to adjust irrigation schedules dynamically.
  • Soil Moisture Sensors
    • Sends signals to the microcontroller to trigger irrigation based on moisture thresholds.
    • Helps in optimizing water usage and preventing over-irrigation.
  • DHT11 Sensor
    • Measures ambient temperature and humidity.
    • Provides digital output, making it easy to interface with microcontrollers.
    • Supports AI-driven irrigation adjustments by supplying real-time environmental data.
  • Water Flow Sensor
    • Tracks the amount of water flowing through the system.
    • Provides accurate water usage data.
  • Solenoid Valves
    • Regulates water flow through the system.
    • Controlled by a 4-channel relay for precise irrigation.
    • Ensures targeted water distribution.
  • Stepdown Module
    • Converts 12V power from an AC-DC adapter to required voltage levels.
    • Ensures stable power supply for ESP32 microcontroller.
    • Protects system integrity through efficient power management.
