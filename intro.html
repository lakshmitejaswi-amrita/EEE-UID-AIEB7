Introduction

In recent years, the agricultural sector has seen a significant transformation through the integration of technology. The Smart Plant Watering System is designed to automate the irrigation process using sensors and microcontrollers. This not only conserves water but also ensures optimal plant health by maintaining appropriate soil moisture levels.

Traditional methods of irrigation often result in overwatering or under-watering, leading to poor crop yield and resource wastage. By automating the watering process, this system provides a sustainable, efficient, and low-maintenance solution for home gardens, greenhouses, and agricultural fields alike.
