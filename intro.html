Introduction

A Smart Plant Watering System using AI and IoT is essential to optimize irrigation, conserve water, and improve plant health.

  • Prevents Overwatering & Underwatering – Sensors ensure plants get the right amount of water.
  • Saves Water & Reduces Waste – Uses only the required amount, preventing excess usage.
  • Saves Time & Effort – Automates watering, reducing manual work.
  • Remote Monitoring & Control – IoT allows users to manage watering via a mobile app
  • Adjusts to Environmental Changes – AI considers temperature, humidity, and rain for smart watering.
  • Improves Plant Growth & Crop Yield – Provides consistent and optimal hydration.
  • Scalable & Customizable – Suitable for home gardens, greenhouses, and large farms.
