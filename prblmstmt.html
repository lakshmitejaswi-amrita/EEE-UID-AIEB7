Problem Statement

  • Inefficient Water Usage: Traditional plant watering methods often result in excessive or insufficient water supply, leading to plant stress and resource wastage.
  • Time-Consuming Manual Monitoring: Regularly checking soil moisture, temperature, humidity, and nutrient levels requires significant effort and is prone to inaccuracies.
  • Limited Remote Access & Control: Farmers and gardeners lack the ability to monitor or adjust irrigation settings remotely, making plant care challenging.
  • Energy Consumption & Sustainability Issues: Many irrigation systems consume excessive electricity, increasing costs and environmental impact.
  • Need for Automation & Smart Decision-Making: A smart system integrating IoT, AI, and real-time data analytics is required to optimize plant health and resource efficiency.
